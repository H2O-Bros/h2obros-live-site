Our Process

1

Surface Assessment

We identify the material (concrete, pavers, stone) and the stains we’re dealing with, then choose the right pressure and technique for each area.

2

Pre-Treatment

We apply cleaning solutions that loosen algae, mildew, and ground-in grime so the wash removes it at the root instead of just the surface.

3

Controlled Pressure Wash

We use a professional surface cleaner for large flat areas — it cleans evenly and avoids the "zebra striping" wand-only jobs leave behind.

4

Spot Treatment & Detail Work

Edges, corners, oil spots, and rust stains get individual attention. We’re honest about what will and won’t come out fully.

5

Rinse-Down & Walkthrough

We rinse surrounding areas clean of debris and walk the job with you (or send photos) to make sure you’re thrilled with the result.
