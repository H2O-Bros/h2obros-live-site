Our Process

1

Walk-Around & Surface Check

We identify your siding type, note delicate areas (window seals, older paint, light fixtures), and plan the wash accordingly.

2

Protect the Landscaping

Plants and grass around the house are pre-wetted and rinsed throughout so the cleaning solution never sits on them.

3

Apply the Soft-Wash Solution

A low-pressure application coats the siding and starts breaking down algae, mildew, and dirt on contact.

4

Dwell & Gentle Rinse

After the solution has done the work, we rinse everything down with gentle pressure — the grime sheets right off.

5

Final Walkthrough

We walk the whole exterior with fresh eyes, touch up any spots, and make sure every side of the house looks its best.
