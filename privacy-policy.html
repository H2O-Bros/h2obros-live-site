Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 18, 2026

H2O Bros (“we,” “us,” or “our”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and share information when you visit h2obros.com, submit forms, call or text us, or otherwise interact with our exterior cleaning business based in Greenville, South Carolina.

Information we collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Contact and quote details: Name, phone number, email address, service address or area, and any details you provide in quote requests, contact forms, or messages.
  • Communications: Information you provide when you call, text, or email us, including scheduling and job-related notes.
  • Website usage data: When you browse our site, our analytics tools may collect technical data such as approximate location (region), device type, browser type, pages viewed, and referring pages. We use Google Analytics (see below).

How we use your information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to quote requests, questions, and scheduling inquiries;
  • Provide and improve our cleaning services;
  • Communicate with you about your project (calls, texts, or email as you prefer);
  • Understand how visitors use our website so we can improve content and user experience;
  • Comply with legal obligations and protect our rights.

Cookies and analytics

Our website uses cookies and similar technologies. We use Google Analytics (Google LLC) to measure traffic and site usage. Google may process data according to its own policies. You can learn more about how Google uses data at Google’s Partner Sites policy, and you may use browser settings or opt-out tools provided by Google to limit certain tracking.

Sharing of information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with:

  • Service providers who help us operate our website, email, or scheduling (for example, hosting or analytics), only as needed to perform those services;
  • Legal or safety reasons when required by law or to protect the rights, property, or safety of H2O Bros, our customers, or others.

Data retention

We retain contact and job-related information only as long as needed to provide services, maintain business records, and meet legal requirements. Analytics data may be retained according to Google’s settings and policies.

Your choices

You may request access to or correction of certain personal information we hold, or ask questions about this policy, by contacting us using the information below. If you are a California resident, you may have additional rights under the CCPA/CPRA; contact us to exercise applicable rights.

Children’s privacy

Our services and website are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the revised policy on this page and update the “Last updated” date.
